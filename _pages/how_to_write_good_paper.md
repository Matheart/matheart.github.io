## How to write a good paper in general?
Begin your paper with a compelling teaser figure that effectively summarizes the core idea. Every figure should be independently informative, accompanied by a clear, concise caption and explicit takeaways, while avoiding excessive white space. Your claims and supporting evidence must be clearly articulated, with evidence robust enough to substantiate each claim. For theory papers, emphasize the key technical innovations that enable new results, as well as the novel implications derived from your findings.
